rootatouille

why rootatouille?

served warm or cold, rootatouille is a delicious side dish with myriad root vegetables.

ingredients

  • potatoes
  • beets
  • onions
  • carrots
  • roasting herbs (thyme, dill)
  • lemon wedge
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 2 tbsp coarse salt

steps

  1. preheat oven to 375F
  2. cut vegetables no larger than 1"
  3. mix with oil, herbs, lemon juice, and salt in large bowl
  4. spread evenly in roasting pan
  5. roast in oven for 40 minutes
  6. remove lid from pan to cook uncovered for 10 minutes
  7. remove pan from oven to sit for 10 minutes
  8. serve.
