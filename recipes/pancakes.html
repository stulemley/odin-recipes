pancakes

why pancakes?

served day or night, pancakes are dope.

ingredients

  • water
  • milk
  • flour
  • 1 tbsp coarse salt
  • baking powder
  • sugar
  • 2 drops vanilla extract

steps

  1. heat skillet on stove to lo-med-lo
  2. mix dry ingredients in bowl
  3. add water, milk, and egg, stir until desired consistency
  4. pour 1 scoop of mix into pan
  5. for quick crepes, gently spread liquid around surface of pan
  6. when edges pucker and small holes present themselves in the cake, flip to other side
  7. cook cake until smooth on bottom
  8. serve
