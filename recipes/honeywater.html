honeywater

why honeywater?

served warm or cold, honeywater is a delicious blend of natural and inexpensive ingredients that soothe your stomach and energize your mind and body without caffeine or other unwanted substances.

ingredients

  • 1 half gallon water
  • 4 tbsp honey
  • 1 tbsp coarse salt
optional
  • 1" ginger root
  • sprig mint or basil
  • 2 lemon wedges

equipment

  • kettle
  • heatsafe glass pitcher

steps

  1. start water to boil in kettle
  2. add salt to bottom of pitcher
  3. pour honey over salt
  4. (opt) squeeze lemons in pitcher, and throw squeezed wedges in as well
  5. toss in ginger and herbs directly in pitcher or in diffuser
  6. when water comes to boil, pour over mixture in pitcher
  7. stir for one minute
